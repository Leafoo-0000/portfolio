# Timeline Archive View Configuration

The explorer page (`archive.html`) presents a comprehensive, structured chronological overview of your academic records.

## 12-Bucket Matrix
- **1st Academic Year** $\rightarrow$ Term 1 | Term 2 | Term 3
- **2nd Academic Year** $\rightarrow$ Term 1 | Term 2 | Term 3
- **3rd Academic Year** $\rightarrow$ Term 1 | Term 2 | Term 3
- **4th Academic Year** $\rightarrow$ Term 1 | Term 2 | Term 3

## Dynamic Mechanics
- **Search Engine Input**: Triggers keyword lookup filters scanning title text, text blocks, and technology tag arrays.
- **Category Filter Dropdown**: Dynamically isolates layout cards based on classification options (`project`, `achievement`, `assessment`).
